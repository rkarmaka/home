The Role of AI in Modern Healthcare

Published on April 11, 2025 · Medical AI Healthcare

Artificial intelligence is transforming the landscape of healthcare — from diagnostics to personalized treatment. In this article, I explore the use of deep learning models in radiology and beyond.

Diagnostic Accuracy

Studies have shown that convolutional neural networks (CNNs) can match, or even exceed, radiologists in certain diagnostic tasks. However, clinical deployment still faces challenges around interpretability and bias.

"AI won't replace doctors — but doctors who use AI will replace those who don't."

Ethical Considerations

With great power comes great responsibility. We need frameworks for evaluating model fairness, patient privacy, and consent when working with AI in medicine.
